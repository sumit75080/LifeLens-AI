LIFELens-AI: Intelligent Kidney Health Monitoring and Assistance

Overview:
LIFELens-AI is an AI-powered healthcare platform designed to assist patients and healthcare providers in monitoring kidney health, analyzing medical scans, and managing personalized health data. It combines intuitive user interfaces, AI analytics, and secure data management to provide actionable insights and recommendations for better kidney care.

Key Features:

User Authentication & Security:

Secure signup and login system with email verification.

Passwords stored safely, ensuring data privacy.

Supports password recovery for user convenience.

Personalized User Dashboard:

After login, users access a comprehensive dashboard displaying key options.

Navigation includes Home, Demographics, Upload & Analyze, Diet Chart, and Reports.

Demographics and Health Tracking:

Users can input personal and health-related data such as age, gender, weight, height, daily water intake, and medical history.

This information helps in tailoring diet charts and health recommendations.

Users can update their demographics, with changes reflected in real-time.

Upload & Analyze Scans:

Patients can upload medical scans (X-rays, ultrasounds, or other relevant images).

Although AI analysis is a future feature, the platform stores the uploads and prepares the system for automatic analysis of kidney conditions.

AI-Driven Diet Recommendations:

Personalized diet plans are suggested based on demographics and health history.

Focus on kidney-friendly nutrition, hydration management, and overall wellness.

Health Reports & Monitoring:

Users can access AI-generated reports derived from medical scan analysis (future integration).

Keeps track of past uploads and provides download options for reports.

User-Friendly Interface:

Clean and intuitive interface built using Streamlit.

Supports background images, interactive forms, and a sidebar navigation menu for easy access.

Secure Data Management:

SQLite database stores all user credentials, demographics, and uploads securely.

Vision:
LIFELens-AI aims to empower both patients and doctors by providing AI-driven insights, monitoring tools, and personalized recommendations. By simplifying health data management and leveraging future AI analytics, it supports early detection and better management of kidney-related health issues.

Potential Future Enhancements:

AI-powered analysis of uploaded scans to detect early signs of kidney diseases.

Integration with wearable devices for real-time monitoring of hydration, blood pressure, and kidney function.

Multilingual support and accessibility features for wider reach.

Mobile app version for on-the-go health monitoring.

In short, LIFELens-AI is like a personal kidney health assistant, combining AI insights, patient data management, and nutrition guidance to enhance kidney care and overall wellness.

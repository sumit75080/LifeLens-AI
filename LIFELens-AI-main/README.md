
LIFE Lens-AI: Kidney Stone Management System 
LIFE Lens-AI is an AI-driven healthcare analytics platform designed to assist in the early prediction, management, and prevention of kidney stone recurrence. By combining machine learning, medical profile analysis, and personalized recommendations, the system aims to support informed clinical decision-making and patient awareness.
This project demonstrates the practical application of Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, and Healthcare Informatics through an interactive web-based solution built using Python and Streamlit. It focuses on predictive risk assessment, lifestyle guidance, and specialist connectivity while maintaining data security and usability.
Project Objectives
Predict kidney stone recurrence risk using AI models
Provide personalized dietary and lifestyle recommendations
Enable long-term patient health tracking
Bridge the gap between patients and medical specialists
Showcase AI-powered healthcare solutions for academic and research purposes
Technology Stack
Frontend: Streamlit
Backend & ML: Python, Scikit-learn
Database: SQLite
AI/Analytics: Machine Learning models for prediction and recommendations
